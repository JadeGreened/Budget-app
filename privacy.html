Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 May 2026

What We Store

Budget App stores budget entries, language preference, and privacy banner choice in your browser localStorage. This information stays on your device.

Why We Store It

The app stores this information so your budget list and preferences remain available after refreshing or reopening the page.

How We Use It

Stored data is used only to run the budget tracker features in your browser. The app does not send your budget entries to a server.

Third Parties

This project does not include advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking scripts.

Your Choices

You can clear stored data at any time through your browser site data settings. You can also reset the privacy banner by clearing localStorage for this site.

Contact

For coursework verification, contact the project team through the GitHub repository used for this submission.
